Introduction

Supply Chain Management is an essential element to operational efficiency. SCM (Supply Chain Management) can be applied to customer satisfaction and company success, it can also help improve quality of life. Basically, the world is a huge supply chain. Supply Chain Management is a system of organizations, people, activities, information, and resources involved in moving a product or service from supplier to customer. The one who integrate all the resource together and make the best combination to other for their product. Supply Chain Management is globally. Most of good companies will require different parts of their product from different place of the world, since the quality and the price. Communication is important for Supply Chain Management. When they are organizing all the resource they will have to communicate to other companies or others. That will take Supply Chain Management do a series of research to get all the information of the target. Usually, the information can be found online, but sometimes in order to get a more detail information face to face meet is necessary. For Supply Chain Management, all kinds of technology will be used in order to get their resource. They will need communication technology such as, Internet resource and people relation to integral their resource. And then the basic transportation such as, fly, boat, train, to deliver their resource to those company which need it. A good using technology is important for all Supply Chain Management.
