Proposed Solution

“Coming together is a beginning. Keeping together is progress. Working together is success,” this is said by Henry Ford (Coming Together, Keeping Together, Working Together). From this we can find that Henry Ford very well highlight the importance of working together in teams. Teamwork refers to the ability of a group, people who have faith in a particular team, in order to support a common goal of mutual cooperation in the process of struggle. It can mobilize all the resources and talents of team members, and will automatically get rid of all the discord and injustice, and it will be given to those sincere and selfless devotee appropriate back reported. If the team is out voluntarily, it will certainly produce a powerful and long-lasting power. Everyone is a part of a team, everyone need to express their ideals, comments. Also have to give up some of their own things for the benefit of the team. A selfish independence could not long-term survival in the team. In order to achieve team’s goals everyone should work together. This is the basis for a person to be able to survive in the team and also the basis of a good team to achieve success.
