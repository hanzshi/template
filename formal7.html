Conclusion

Overall, the mission of Supply Chain Management is that using the lowest cost to satisfied the customers’ requirements. The system of Supply Chain is setting up on the basic of the customers. Customer service is one of powerful factor for an enterprise gain competitive advantage. The level of customer service directly reflects the enterprise’s ability of design and manage their own supply chain system size. Basically, the world is a huge supply chain. Supply Chain Management is a system of organizations, people, activities, information, and resources involved in moving a product or service from supplier to customer. The one who integrate all the resource together and make the best combination to other for their product. Supply Chain Management is globally.
