Conclusion

Overall, teamwork is very important to everything. In World of Warcraft, teamwork can be the key lead to the victory. In daily life, teamwork can be a short cut to achieve success. Everyone in the team should not only think about himself but think more about the group benefit. Teamwork can bring all the resource together and make a utilization, create a better creative and learning environment and also will increase the chance to communicate with others to get a better relationship between each other. Individual work will not always achieve people’s goal but unite everyone’s power together and that will bring the world a surprise.
