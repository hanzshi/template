Remix Memo

This Multimedia Remix Project is going to show the effects of the highly technology will bring to people’s life especial the current generation. Our idea is coming from Jenna’s Cultural analysis paper. In nowadays the technology is developing rapidly, and everyone everyday have to face various of technology. As a result the technology has became overloaded. So our documentary is going to propose that the current generation should use less and less technology.

Our team has a group chat which we can keep contact with each other while someone of us find some good resource and shared on the D2L working area, others can check the resources and give suggestions. In order to finish this project our group has meet up twice in the main library. We discussed how our video’s structure suppose to be and what kind of video link or pictures we supposed to use in our documentary.
